About ARX Construction

Our Story

From Foundation to Skyline

Founded in 2005 by a team of visionary engineers, ARX Construction Ltd set out with a singular mission: to redefine quality in the construction industry. What began as a regional contractor has grown into a full-service construction powerhouse with over 500 completed projects across commercial, industrial, residential, and infrastructure sectors.

Our name — ARX — derives from the Latin word for citadel or stronghold, a reflection of our commitment to building structures that endure, protect, and inspire for generations to come.

🎯 Our Mission

To deliver construction excellence that transforms communities, empowers clients, and upholds the highest standards of safety, sustainability, and innovation in every project we undertake.
